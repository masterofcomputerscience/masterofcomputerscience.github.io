VISION

This platform has been created to support students preparing for IGCSE Information & Communication Technology (0417), IGCSE Computer Science (0478) and A-Level Computer Science (9618)

Over the years, I have seen that one of the biggest challenges students face is not a lack of effort — but a lack of the right resources in one place. Past papers, marking schemes, and clear notes are often scattered, making preparation unnecessarily difficult.

LearnWithASQ is built to simplify that process.

Here, you will find:

  • Organized past papers
  • Marking schemes
  • Carefully prepared notes
  • Materials designed to help you understand how questions are asked and how answers are evaluated

The goal is simple:
To make exam preparation more structured, more focused, and more effective.

This website is not about shortcuts. It is about helping you practice properly, understand expectations, and improve with consistency. Use these resources regularly, stay disciplined, and you will see the difference in your performance.

— Ali Shehroze Qazalbash
