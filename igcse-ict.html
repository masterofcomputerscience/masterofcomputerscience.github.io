IGCSE ICT 0417. Technology Is Everywhere. Learn to Master It.

📘 Course Overview

Every photo you edit. Every spreadsheet that runs a business. Every website you browse. Every database that stores your information — behind all of it is someone who understood how technology works.

That person could be you.

Cambridge IGCSE ICT (0417) transforms everyday users into confident, skilled, and critically aware digital professionals. It connects classroom learning directly to the real world — from offices to media studios to online businesses.

This isn’t about complex coding. It’s about understanding the full digital landscape — and knowing how to use and shape it.

🌍 What You'll Explore

  • The Digital World: How technology operates in society and business
  • Spreadsheets & Data Handling: Analyse and present data effectively
  • Document Production: Create professional reports and publications
  • Databases: Store, organise, and retrieve data efficiently
  • Web Authoring: Build and understand websites
  • Multimedia: Create presentations using media elements
  • Security & Ethics: Stay safe and responsible in the digital world

📝 Assessment Structure

  • Paper 1: Theory (2 hours)
  • Paper 2: Practical Skills (2 hours 30 minutes)
  • Paper 3: Coursework / Practical Test (2 hours 30 minutes)

🚀 Why Choose IGCSE ICT?

The world runs on technology — and the people who succeed aren’t just users, but creators and problem-solvers. IGCSE ICT builds practical skills that apply to every career and every digital environment.

From managing data to creating digital content, the skills you gain here are instantly useful and widely applicable.

Technology isn’t the future. It’s the present. And this is where you learn to own it.
