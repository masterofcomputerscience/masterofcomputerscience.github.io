📘 Course Overview

Ever wondered what happens the moment you hit "send"? How a search engine finds results in milliseconds? How a game tracks your every move? The answers lie in Cambridge IGCSE Computer Science (0478).

This is more than a subject — it's your entry into one of the most powerful disciplines of the modern world. You’ll develop logical thinking, creative problem-solving, and a deep understanding of the technology shaping everyday life.

You don’t just learn about computers. You learn to think like one — and then surpass one.

🧠 What You'll Explore

  • Problem-Solving & Algorithms: Break down complex problems into logical steps
  • Data Representation: Learn how computers store and process data
  • Computer Architecture: Understand how hardware works internally
  • Networks & the Internet: Explore global communication systems
  • Cybersecurity: Learn how systems are protected from threats
  • Programming: Write real code and bring ideas to life

📝 Assessment Structure

  • Paper 1: Theory (1 hour 45 minutes)
  • Paper 2: Problem-Solving & Programming (1 hour 45 minutes)
  • Paper 3: Practical (1 hour 20 minutes)

🚀 Why Choose IGCSE Computer Science?

In a world driven by data and technology, computer science is essential. This course builds skills that go beyond exams — preparing you for university, careers, and real-world problem solving.

Whether you want to build apps, design games, secure systems, or simply understand technology — this is where your journey begins.
