ABOUT

I am a Computer Science educator who believes that success in examinations is not achieved through volume of study, but through clarity of understanding and purposeful practice. Over the years, I have worked closely with students preparing for IGCSE and A-Level examinations, and I have observed a common challenge — students often put in significant effort, yet struggle to translate that effort into results. This is rarely due to a lack of ability, but rather a lack of structured guidance and a clear understanding of how examinations are designed and assessed. My approach focuses on simplifying complex concepts, developing strong exam awareness, and helping students understand not just *what* to study, but *how* to apply their knowledge effectively. I place particular emphasis on interpreting questions accurately, recognising patterns in past papers, and aligning answers with marking schemes. LearnWithASQ is an extension of this philosophy — a space where students can access the right resources, practice with direction, and build confidence through consistency. The aim is not only to improve grades, but to develop a disciplined and focused approach to learning that stays with students beyond the examination.
